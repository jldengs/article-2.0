OUTSTANDING FACILITIES

CTU FACILITY CENTRUM

The establishment of the CTU Facility Centrum represents a strategic investment in the future of education and community engagement in the Danao region. By providing cutting-edge resources and a welcoming space for accommodation and events, the Centrum enhances the academic capabilities of Cebu Technological University while generating revenue that can be reinvested into the institution's development. This revenue stream is vital for funding research, upgrading facilities, and expanding educational programs, ultimately equipping students with the skills necessary to thrive in a competitive workforce. Additionally, the Centrum fosters partnerships with local industries and organizations, contributing to economic development through research, innovation initiatives, and collaborative events that benefit both the university and the surrounding community.

STUDENT ACTIVITY CENTER

The Student Activity Center at Cebu Technological University-Danao Campus serves as a vibrant hub for student engagement and community interaction. Designed to accommodate a wide range of activities, this modern facility is equipped with versatile spaces suitable for school events, such as seminars, workshops, and cultural presentations, as well as for external customers looking to host special occasions. Whether it’s a formal gathering, a social event, or a community celebration, the center provides an inviting atmosphere that caters to diverse needs. Its strategic location and modern amenities make it an ideal venue for fostering collaboration and creativity among students and guests alike. The Student Activity Center not only enhances the university experience for its students but also opens its doors to the community, reinforcing the institution's commitment to being a proactive participant in local events and activities.

RAMON M. DURANO SR. MEMORIAL SPORTS COMPLEX

The Ramon M. Durano Memorial Sports Complex at Cebu Technological University-Danao Campus stands as a premier facility dedicated to sports, recreation, and community events. This expansive complex is designed to host a variety of activities, making it a vital asset for both school and external customers. It serves as a venue for school competitions, athletic training, and physical education programs, while also welcoming guests for special events such as sports tournaments, festivals, and concerts. The facility boasts ample space, accommodating large crowds and providing an inclusive environment for everyone to enjoy. Its modern amenities and well-maintained grounds make it one of the highlight facilities of the university, showcasing CTU-Danao’s commitment to promoting physical fitness, community engagement, and cultural celebrations. The Ramon M. Durano Memorial Sports Complex not only enhances the student experience but also strengthens the university's role as a hub for community activities and events.
